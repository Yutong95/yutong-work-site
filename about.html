Yutong Liu / 刘禹桐

Yutong Liu is an independent graphic and visual designer from China. Her work based on machine sympathy, research on material and immaterial, visual languages using, exploration on printed and digital publication. Her MFA is Design - Visual Communication at Virginia Commonwealth University School of Fine Arts in USA (2018-2020)🇺🇸. Her BFA is Visual Communication Design at Luxun Academy School of Fine Arts in China (2013-2017)🇨🇳.

刘禹桐，来自中国的独立平面与视觉设计师，她的作品相关于机械情感共鸣，材料性与非材料性的研究，视觉语言的应用，印刷和数字出版物的探索。她的研究生专业是设计与视觉传达在弗吉尼亚联邦大学艺术学院（2018-2020）。她的本科专业是视觉传达设计在中国鲁迅美术学院（2013-2017)。

Feel free to say hi and contact her for any freelance works or projects.
