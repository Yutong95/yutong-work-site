感谢有你 Thanks For You



This animation also won the Second-Class Award of Mainland Group in Times Young Creative Awards in 2016.

The main characters of this animation are dumplings which mean purse in Chinese culture. The ‘Baidu purse’ is an APP which can pay and manage the money, order the food, see the movies and buy the travel ticket. In our life, it is our assistants. In this animation, the Baidu purse plays the role of husband or family and emphasize the warm-hearted and sense of security for the user.

这部动画片的主角是饺子，饺子在中国文化中是钱包的意思。“百度钱包”是一款可以支付和管理费用、点餐、看电影和购买车票的应用程序。在我们的生活中，它是我们的助手。在这个动画中，百度钱包扮演了丈夫或家庭重要成员的角色，强调了用户的温暖和使用安全感。
