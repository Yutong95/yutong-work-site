“As a transformer” Surface Workshop



⭐️

With collage art as the center, the students will be guided to explore the pleasure of material collection, classification, combination, reorganization and transformation in collage art. This workshop not only brings students the pleasure of seeking for the transformation of artistic creation, but also aims to cultivate students' habit of collecting and reorganizing materials to inspire inspiration, so as to make students fall in love with artistic creation.
